Blue Springs Remodeling

Upgrading the Homes That Built Eastern Jackson County

Blue Springs sits on the eastern edge of the Kansas City metro, a community that has maintained its own distinct identity even as the metro has grown around it. Named for the natural springs that once drew settlers along the Santa Fe Trail, the city today is known for its strong school district, its annual Fall Fun Festival, and neighborhoods where people know their neighbors by name. The housing stock reflects decades of steady growth — from modest ranch homes near downtown and along Woods Chapel Road to larger two-story builds in Adams Dairy Landing, Lakewood, and the developments surrounding Fleming Park.

Many Blue Springs homes built in the 1980s and 1990s carry the hallmarks of their era: oak cabinetry, Formica counters, brass fixtures, and carpeted bathrooms. These homes were well-built, but tastes and daily routines have changed. Families want open kitchens where they can watch the kids while cooking, bathrooms that feel like a retreat rather than an afterthought, and basements that serve a purpose beyond storing holiday decorations. The rolling terrain east of the city also means many homes have walkout basements with real daylight potential.

Uprise Remodeling serves Blue Springs from our base in Lenexa, KS, bringing the same attention to detail and transparent pricing that has earned us 41+ five-star reviews since 2021. We handle all permitting through the City of Blue Springs, communicate in English or Spanish, and deliver written, itemized proposals so there are never surprises on your invoice.

What We Do in Blue Springs

Our Blue Springs Remodeling Services

Kitchen Remodels

Blue Springs kitchens from the 1980s and 1990s often feature closed layouts with dated oak and brass. We open up walls, install modern cabinetry and stone countertops, and add pendant lighting that transforms the heart of your home into a bright, functional gathering space.

Bathroom Remodels

Carpeted bathrooms and builder-grade tub surrounds are still common in Blue Springs. We replace them with porcelain tile, frameless glass showers, and updated vanities that bring comfort and style to your morning routine.

Basement Finishes

Blue Springs' hilly terrain gives many homes walkout basements with natural light and patio access. We finish these lower levels into family rooms, game areas, or guest suites that feel like a natural extension of the main floor — not an afterthought.

Outdoor Projects

With Fleming Park and Blue Springs Lake right in your backyard, outdoor living is second nature here. We build cedar and composite decks, screened porches, and fire pit patios designed for long Missouri evenings under the stars.
