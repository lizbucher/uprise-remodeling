Built Because KC Homeowners Deserved Better

Uprise Remodeling & Design started in 2021 because KC metro homeowners deserved better. Better communication. Better craftsmanship. Better follow-through. Too many contractors in this market take deposits, miss deadlines, dodge calls, and deliver mediocre work. Uprise was built to be the opposite of that experience.

We're a family-owned remodeling company based in Lenexa, Kansas, and we serve homeowners across Overland Park, Olathe, Kansas City (KS & MO), De Soto, Spring Hill, Bonner Springs, Leavenworth, Edwardsville, Eudora, and Belton.
