Kansas City MO Remodeling

Modern Living Inside KCMO's Character-Rich Homes

Kansas City, Missouri is the heart of the metro — a city of fountains, barbecue, jazz history, and neighborhoods with unmistakable personality. Brookside's tree-lined streets are filled with 1920s Tudor and Colonial homes. Waldo offers affordable ranches and Cape Cods that first-time buyers snap up. The Northland stretches from Gladstone to Platte County with split-levels and newer two-story builds. South KC, Midtown, and the Country Club Plaza area feature everything from historic mansions to charming duplexes. No two KCMO neighborhoods are alike, and no remodel should be either.

The challenge in many KCMO homes is balancing preservation with modernization. A 1940s Brookside kitchen with its original plaster walls and wood trim has charm, but it may also have outdated wiring, poor ventilation, and a layout that predates the open-concept era by decades. Waldo homeowners often face single-bathroom floor plans that need a second bath added. Northland families frequently have large unfinished basements in homes built during the 1990s and 2000s expansion. Each scenario requires a different approach, and that is where our experience matters.

Uprise Remodeling crosses the state line from our Lenexa, KS office to serve KCMO with the same quality and transparency that has earned us 41+ five-star reviews. We navigate KCMO's permitting process — which runs through the city's Codes Administration — and provide written, itemized proposals in English or Spanish. Whether your home sits on a quiet Brookside block or a Northland cul-de-sac, we bring the same dedication to every project.

What We Do in Kansas City, MO

Our KCMO Remodeling Services

Kitchen Remodels

KCMO kitchens vary wildly — from tiny galley layouts in Midtown apartments to oversized but dated spaces in Northland homes. We design solutions specific to your home's era and architecture, from careful wall removals in Brookside Tudors to full-scale updates in newer south-side builds.

Bathroom Remodels

Many classic KCMO homes were built with just one bathroom. We add second baths where possible and transform existing ones with modern tile, updated plumbing, and fixtures that respect the home's original style while delivering today's comfort.

Basement Finishes

KCMO basements range from stone-walled cellars in century-old homes to poured-concrete walkouts in the Northland. We assess moisture, structural needs, and light conditions before designing finished spaces that add real livable square footage to your home.

Outdoor Projects

KCMO's four distinct seasons make outdoor spaces a year-round investment. We build screened porches for spring storms, shade pergolas for July heat, fire pit patios for October evenings, and covered structures that extend your outdoor season by months.
