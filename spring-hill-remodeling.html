Spring Hill Remodeling

Making New Homes Feel Like Forever Homes

Spring Hill has transformed from a quiet farming community straddling the Johnson and Miami county line into one of the fastest-growing cities in the entire state of Kansas. The Spring Hill School District's strong reputation, relatively affordable land, and easy access to both Olathe and the southern KC metro have drawn waves of young families over the past two decades. New subdivisions continue to rise along 199th Street, Webster Street, and the corridors branching off US-169, bringing fresh rooftops to what was recently open prairie.

The housing in Spring Hill is overwhelmingly newer — built from the mid-2000s through today — which means the bones are sound but the finishes are often production-grade. Flat-panel cabinets, laminate countertops, basic tile work in bathrooms, and vinyl plank flooring throughout are standard in many Spring Hill homes. These materials serve their purpose for the first few years, but families who plan to stay begin to notice the difference between builder-standard and the custom touches that make a house feel truly finished. Unfinished basements are nearly universal, representing hundreds of square feet of untapped potential.

Uprise Remodeling serves Spring Hill from our Lenexa office, a straight drive south on US-169 or down through Olathe. We handle permitting through the City of Spring Hill and Johnson County, communicate in English or Spanish, and deliver the same written, itemized proposals that have earned us 41+ five-star reviews since 2021. For a community that is building its future, we are the remodeling team that helps you build yours.

What We Do in Spring Hill

Our Spring Hill Remodeling Services

Kitchen Remodels

Spring Hill kitchens are often spacious but finished with basic builder materials that start showing wear within a few years. We upgrade countertops to quartz or granite, swap in soft-close cabinetry, and add design details like tile backsplashes and under-cabinet lighting that make your kitchen feel custom.

Bathroom Remodels

Builder-grade master baths in Spring Hill often come with basic fiberglass shower pans and simple vanities. We install tiled showers with niches and bench seats, upgrade to double vanities with stone tops, and add finishes that turn your bathroom into a retreat.

Basement Finishes

Almost every Spring Hill home has an unfinished basement waiting for a purpose. We design playrooms for growing families, home theaters for movie nights, guest bedrooms with full baths, and rec rooms with wet bars — all built to the same standard as your main floor.

Outdoor Projects

Spring Hill's wide open lots and Kansas sunsets are made for outdoor living. We build composite decks, covered pergolas, and outdoor kitchens that give your family a reason to put down the screens and enjoy the backyard together.
