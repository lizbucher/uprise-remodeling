Bathroom Remodels Built Around How You Actually Live

Custom tile showers, double vanities, heated flooring. Designed for your routine, not a template.

Full-Service Bathroom Remodels

Rebuild the Room You Use Twice a Day

You use your bathroom twice a day at minimum — and if it's cramped, outdated, or falling apart, that frustration compounds. The grout that won't come clean. The showerhead that barely works. The vanity that two people can't use at the same time.

We rebuild bathrooms from the studs out, designing around how you actually use the space — not just how it looks in a photo.

What's Included

  • Full bathroom demolition and removal
  • Layout redesign and space optimization
  • Custom tile shower and tub surrounds
  • Vanity selection and installation (single or double)
  • Plumbing relocation and fixture upgrades
  • Heated flooring installation
  • Lighting design and electrical updates
  • Mirror, hardware, and accessory installation
  • Ventilation and exhaust fan upgrades
  • Waterproofing and moisture barrier systems
  • All permits pulled and inspections managed by Uprise
  • Final walkthrough and punch list completion
