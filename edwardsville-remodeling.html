Edwardsville Remodeling

Making Edwardsville Homes Work Harder for the Families Inside Them

Edwardsville is one of those Wyandotte County gems that people outside the area rarely hear about — but residents wouldn't trade it. Nestled between Bonner Springs and Kansas City, KS, this small community along K-32 and Riverview Avenue offers something rare in the metro: affordable homes on generous lots with a genuine rural-suburban feel, all within a 20-minute commute of downtown KC. The housing here is a mix of older farmhouse-style properties, modest mid-century ranches, and a handful of newer builds that have gone up as families discover the area's value.

Many Edwardsville homes were built in the 1950s through 1970s and share common traits: solid construction but dated interiors, original single-pane windows, kitchens with limited cabinet and counter space, and bathrooms that haven't been updated since they were built. Basements are often partially finished or used strictly for storage, representing a significant amount of untapped square footage. With the area's proximity to the Kansas River, proper drainage and moisture barriers are essential before any basement project — and that's a detail we never skip.

Uprise Remodeling is based in Lenexa, making Edwardsville an easy service area for our crews. We understand the building requirements under Wyandotte County's Unified Government, and we pull every permit so your renovation is fully compliant and inspected. Our bilingual team communicates clearly in English and Spanish, and our written, itemized proposals mean there are never surprises on your invoice. For a community that values honest work and fair dealing, we think that's the right way to do business.

What We Do in Edwardsville

Our Edwardsville Remodeling Services

Kitchen Remodels

Edwardsville kitchens tend to be compact and builder-basic, with laminate counters and limited storage. We redesign these spaces with custom cabinetry, durable countertops, and smarter layouts that give you room to cook, gather, and enjoy your home.

Bathroom Remodels

Whether your Edwardsville home has a single full bath or a master that needs a complete overhaul, we handle it all — from updated plumbing and tile work to new vanities and walk-in showers that make your morning routine easier.

Basement Finishes

With lot sizes in Edwardsville often exceeding what you'd find closer to KC, many homes here have full, deep basements begging to be finished. We start with moisture management — critical near the Kansas River — then frame, wire, and finish your new family room, office, or guest suite.

Outdoor Projects

Edwardsville's spacious lots and quiet streets make it ideal for outdoor living. We build custom decks, covered patios, and outdoor cooking areas so you can make the most of your yard from spring through fall.
