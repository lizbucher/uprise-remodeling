Full-Service Outdoor Projects

Your Backyard Should Be an Extension of Your Home

Kansas City summers are too short to waste on a backyard that doesn't work. A deck that's rotting. A fence that's leaning. No shade. No privacy. No reason to go outside.

We build outdoor living spaces designed to hold up through July humidity and January ice — structures that look good, function better, and give you a reason to stay outside longer.

What's Included

  • Custom deck design and construction (composite and wood)
  • Pergola and shade structure installation
  • Fence installation and replacement (wood, vinyl, composite)
  • Screened-in porch construction
  • Exterior painting (full home, trim, accent)
  • Wood rot repair and structural reinforcement
  • Railing systems and stair construction
  • Outdoor lighting
  • All building and construction permits handled by Uprise
  • Final walkthrough and inspection sign-off
