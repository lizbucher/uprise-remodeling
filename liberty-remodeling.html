Liberty Remodeling

Premium Remodeling for the Northland's Crown Jewel

Liberty is the kind of place people move to and never leave. As the Clay County seat and home to William Jewell College, it combines a vibrant historic downtown — anchored by the Liberty Square with its shops, restaurants, and the iconic 1858 jail museum — with some of the strongest residential neighborhoods in the Northland. The streets surrounding the Square and stretching south along Lightburne and Mississippi are lined with beautifully maintained Victorians, Colonials, and Craftsman bungalows, many dating to the late 1800s and early 1900s. These homes are full of character — original millwork, hardwood floors, stone foundations — but their kitchens and bathrooms often reflect the last time they were touched, which may have been the 1960s or earlier.

North and east of the historic core, Liberty has experienced significant growth over the past two decades. Subdivisions like Liberty Hills, Westboro, Ridgeview, and the neighborhoods along 291 Highway have brought modern two-story and reverse-1.5-story homes to the area, many built between 2000 and 2015. These homes attracted families with their good school districts (Liberty Public Schools is consistently ranked among the top in Missouri) and reasonable price points, but after 10 to 20 years of daily life, builder-grade kitchens, carpet-heavy floor plans, and unfinished basements are ready for their next evolution.

Uprise Remodeling serves Liberty from our Lenexa base across the metro. We're experienced with Clay County's building permit process and handle every filing and inspection on your behalf. Our bilingual team delivers written, itemized proposals so you know exactly what your project will cost before we begin. With 41+ five-star reviews earned since our 2021 founding, we've built our reputation on meticulous craftsmanship and clear communication — exactly the standard Liberty homeowners expect.

What We Do in Liberty

Our Liberty Remodeling Services

Kitchen Remodels

Liberty kitchens range from the charming but cramped layouts in historic homes near the Square to the open-concept but builder-basic setups in newer subdivisions. We tailor each design to your home's architecture — whether that means preserving period details or replacing stock materials with custom cabinetry and stone countertops.

Bathroom Remodels

Liberty's newer homes often have master baths with oversized garden tubs that go unused. We convert these into spacious walk-in showers with custom tile and frameless glass. In older Liberty homes, we upgrade single baths with modern fixtures, improved ventilation, and timeless finishes.

Basement Finishes

Many Liberty homes — especially in the subdivisions built since 2000 — have full unfinished basements with roughed-in plumbing and high ceilings. We transform these spaces into family entertainment areas, wet bars, home offices, or guest suites that add significant livable square footage.

Outdoor Projects

Liberty's hilly terrain creates interesting backyard elevations that are perfect for multi-level decks and terraced patios. We design outdoor living spaces — including grilling stations and pergolas — that take advantage of your lot's natural contours and extend your entertaining season.
