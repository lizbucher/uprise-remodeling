De Soto Remodeling

Bringing Big-City Quality to De Soto's Quiet Streets

De Soto sits along the Kansas River in western Johnson County, a small city that has long offered a quieter alternative to the busier suburbs to the east. With a population that has grown steadily as the Panasonic manufacturing campus and surrounding development bring new energy to the area, De Soto is experiencing a level of attention it has never seen before. Longtime residents who bought acreage homes and farmstead properties years ago are now sitting on increasingly valuable land, while new arrivals are building and buying in the developments taking shape along the K-10 corridor.

The housing in De Soto reflects its transitional character. You will find older ranch homes and split-levels from the 1970s and 1980s along 83rd Street and Kill Creek Road, many with original kitchens and bathrooms that have served their families well but are ready for a refresh. Newer construction in subdivisions near Sunflower Road and Lexington Avenue offers more modern floor plans but still often comes with builder-grade finishes — basic cabinets, laminate counters, and unfinished basements that represent significant untapped potential.

Uprise Remodeling is based just east in Lenexa, making De Soto an easy drive down K-10. We handle permits through the City of De Soto and Johnson County, provide written proposals, and communicate in English or Spanish. Our 41+ five-star reviews since 2021 reflect a commitment to quality that De Soto homeowners — whether longtime residents or new arrivals — can count on.

What We Do in De Soto

Our De Soto Remodeling Services

Kitchen Remodels

De Soto's older ranch homes often have kitchens walled off from the rest of the house. We open up these spaces, install custom cabinetry and stone countertops, and create kitchens built for the way rural and suburban families cook — big meals, big gatherings, big counter space.

Bathroom Remodels

Whether you have a single outdated bathroom in a 1970s ranch or a builder-grade master bath in a newer home, we design bathrooms with quality tile, modern vanities, and walk-in showers that make a real difference in your daily routine.

Basement Finishes

De Soto homes on acreage or hillside lots often have full walkout basements with stunning views. We finish these spaces into family retreats, workshops, or in-law suites that take advantage of the natural setting surrounding your property.

Outdoor Projects

De Soto's rural character means bigger yards and more room to build. We create expansive decks, covered patios, and outdoor kitchens that let you enjoy the open skies and green space that drew you to this part of Johnson County in the first place.
