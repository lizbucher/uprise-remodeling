Belton Remodeling

Upgrading Belton Homes from Markey Park to the South 71 Corridor

Belton occupies a sweet spot in the southern KC metro — close enough to downtown Kansas City for an easy commute along 71 Highway or I-49, but far enough south that you get bigger yards, quieter streets, and a genuine sense of community. The town's roots as a rail hub and agricultural center are still visible in the older neighborhoods near downtown along Main Street and around Markey Park, where you'll find Craftsman-era cottages and post-war bungalows with hardwood floors, covered porches, and kitchens that haven't kept pace with how families cook and gather today.

East of 71 Highway and along the Y Highway corridor, the landscape shifts to 1980s and 1990s subdivisions — split-levels, bi-levels, and traditional two-stories that were affordable when they were built and remain popular with first-time buyers and growing families. These homes commonly have builder-grade finishes, laminate countertops that are showing their age, hall bathrooms with fiberglass tub-shower combos, and partially finished basements that were left as blank canvases. Belton's proximity to the Belton-Grandview-Hickman Mills area means property values reward smart renovations, making a kitchen or basement remodel a strong investment.

Uprise Remodeling serves Belton from our Lenexa headquarters, and we're well-versed in Cass County's permitting requirements for residential remodeling. We pull every permit, schedule every inspection, and deliver written, itemized proposals so there are no surprises. Our bilingual team communicates in English and Spanish, and our 41+ five-star reviews reflect the kind of honest, detail-oriented work that Belton homeowners expect.

What We Do in Belton

Our Belton Remodeling Services

Kitchen Remodels

Belton's split-level and bi-level homes often have kitchens that open directly to a dining area but feel cramped and dated. We reconfigure counters, upgrade to soft-close cabinetry, and install stone or quartz surfaces that transform these hardworking kitchens into spaces worth gathering in.

Bathroom Remodels

From the fiberglass shower surrounds common in Belton's 80s and 90s homes to the tiny half-baths tucked under stairways, we upgrade every bathroom to feel modern, durable, and comfortable. Custom tile, new vanities, and proper ventilation make a real difference.

Basement Finishes

Many Belton homes have full basements that are partially framed or used only for storage. We finish these spaces into family rooms, home theaters, workout areas, or guest bedrooms — adding real square footage and real value to your home.

Outdoor Projects

Belton's spacious lots — especially in the neighborhoods east of 71 — give you room for a proper outdoor living area. We build decks, covered patios, and grilling stations that extend your usable space from April through October.
