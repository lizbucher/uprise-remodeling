Most kitchen remodels take 6–12 weeks from demo to completion. The timeline depends on scope, material lead times (custom cabinetry can take 4–6 weeks), and the city's permit schedule. We provide a detailed week-by-week timeline at the proposal stage.

Bathroom remodels typically take 3–6 weeks. Smaller guest bath updates can sometimes be completed in under 3 weeks, while full master bath gut renovations tend toward the 5–6 week range.
