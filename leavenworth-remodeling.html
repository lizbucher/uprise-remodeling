Leavenworth Remodeling

Renovations Rooted in Leavenworth's Rich Architectural Heritage

Leavenworth is one of the oldest cities in Kansas, and its residential architecture reflects more than 160 years of history. The neighborhoods surrounding downtown — along Limit Street, Osage Street, and the tree-lined blocks near Carroll Park — feature some of the finest Victorian, Queen Anne, and Craftsman homes in the region. These houses have extraordinary character: original woodwork, plaster walls, high ceilings, and porches that wrap the entire front. But their kitchens and bathrooms often remain stuck in the 1950s or earlier, with outdated wiring, small fixtures, and layouts that don't serve modern families.

South of downtown toward Fort Leavenworth and the VA campus, you'll find mid-century ranch homes and Cape Cods built during the post-war housing boom. These are solid, well-built houses, but they typically have single bathrooms, cramped kitchens with limited counter space, and unfinished basements that represent hundreds of square feet of wasted potential. The neighborhoods along Eisenhower Road and near Haymarket Square have seen renewed interest from young families and military personnel, making smart remodeling a sound investment.

Uprise Remodeling brings the same attention to detail that Leavenworth's historic homes demand. We understand how to work with plaster and lath walls, how to modernize kitchens in older floor plans without gutting their character, and how to navigate Leavenworth County permitting requirements. Our bilingual team has earned 41+ five-star reviews across the metro, and we're committed to transparent, written proposals so you always know exactly what your project will cost before we start.

What We Do in Leavenworth

Our Leavenworth Remodeling Services

Kitchen Remodels

Leavenworth's older homes often have kitchens tucked into small back rooms, disconnected from the rest of the house. We reconfigure layouts, add modern cabinetry, and blend new materials with your home's existing character for a kitchen that feels both timeless and functional.

Bathroom Remodels

Many historic Leavenworth bathrooms have cast-iron tubs and pedestal sinks that charm but don't perform. We upgrade plumbing, install walk-in showers, and add storage without sacrificing the period details that make your home special.

Basement Finishes

Leavenworth basements — especially in the older hilltop neighborhoods — can have stone or limestone foundations that need careful moisture management. We address water intrusion first, then build out comfortable living space you'll actually use year-round.

Outdoor Projects

With Leavenworth's stunning Missouri River bluff views and deep, shaded lots, outdoor living space is a natural extension of your home. We design and build decks, patios, and pergolas that complement the architectural style of your property.
