About Uprise

Built on Trust. Backed by Results.

Uprise Remodeling & Design was founded in 2021 with a simple idea: homeowners deserve a remodeler who communicates clearly, shows up on time, and delivers exactly what was promised. Five years and 41+ five-star reviews later, that idea hasn't changed — it's just proven.

We're a family-owned team based in Lenexa, KS, serving homeowners across Overland Park, Olathe, Kansas City, De Soto, Spring Hill, and the entire KC metro. We speak English and Spanish. We handle every permit. And we don't consider a project finished until you walk through it and say it's right.
