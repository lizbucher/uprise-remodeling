Full-Service Basement Finishes

Unlock the Biggest Unused Room in Your House

Your unfinished basement is the biggest untapped room in your house. Right now it's storing holiday decorations and dust. It could be a home theater, a guest suite, a home office, a gym, a wet bar, a kids' playroom — or all of the above.

Basement finishing adds livable square footage to your home without building an addition, and it's one of the highest-ROI remodeling projects you can do.

What's Included

  • Design and space planning
  • Full framing and insulation
  • Drywall installation and finishing
  • Electrical wiring (outlets, lighting, dedicated circuits)
  • Plumbing (wet bars, bathrooms, laundry hookups)
  • Flooring installation (LVP, carpet, tile)
  • Egress window installation (required by code for bedrooms)
  • HVAC duct extensions and climate control
  • Built-in shelving and custom storage
  • Trim, painting, and finishing
  • All building permits pulled and managed by Uprise
  • Final walkthrough and inspection sign-off
