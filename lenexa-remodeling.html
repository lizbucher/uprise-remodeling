Lenexa Remodeling

Remodeling the Homes We Live Among

Lenexa has grown from a small farming community along the Santa Fe Trail into one of Johnson County's most desirable addresses. From the established split-levels and ranch homes near Old Town Lenexa to the newer two-story builds in neighborhoods like Falcon Ridge, The Villas at Lenexa, and Westbrooke Green, the housing stock here spans decades of architectural style. That variety means every remodel starts with understanding the specific bones of your home.

Many Lenexa homes built in the 1970s and 1980s around Pflumm Road and 87th Street still carry original galley kitchens and single-vanity bathrooms that no longer fit how families live today. Meanwhile, the growing City Center district is attracting new residents who want modern, open-plan finishes from the start. Whether your home overlooks Shawnee Mission Park or sits near the bustle of K-10 Highway, we tailor every project to the way you actually use your space.

Because our office is at 8855 Long Street, a Lenexa project means zero drive time and fast response. We know the Johnson County permitting process inside and out, and we have built lasting relationships with local inspectors. Uprise Remodeling was founded right here in 2021, and we are proud that more than 41 five-star reviews reflect the trust our own community has placed in us.

What We Do in Lenexa

Our Lenexa Remodeling Services

Kitchen Remodels

Many Lenexa kitchens built in the 1970s-80s feature closed-off galley layouts that beg to be opened up. We remove walls, install custom cabinetry, and add quartz or granite countertops so your kitchen finally fits the way your family cooks and gathers.

Bathroom Remodels

From outdated builder-grade hall baths to cramped master suites, Lenexa bathrooms are often the first room homeowners want to upgrade. We design walk-in showers, double vanities, and heated tile floors that transform your daily routine.

Basement Finishes

Lenexa basements are typically generous in square footage but underused. We turn that raw concrete into finished rec rooms, home theaters, wet bars, and guest suites that effectively double your livable space.

Outdoor Projects

With Lenexa's trail system and green spaces right outside your door, outdoor living is a natural extension of your home. We build composite decks, stamped-concrete patios, and full outdoor kitchens designed to handle Kansas summers and falls.
